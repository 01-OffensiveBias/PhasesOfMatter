Plasma

The plasma phase of matter as demonstrated above with the larger dots representing nuclei and the smaller dots representing electrons (look closely). Plasma is a phase of matter with the most molecular energy out of the 4 common phases. Plasma has so much molecular energy that the electrons break off from their orbits around the nuclei and float freely.
