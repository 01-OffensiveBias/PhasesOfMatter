Liquid

The liquid phase of matter as demonstrated above is a phase of matter where the molecules have more energy than solids but less than gases. A result of this, is that the molecules aren't bounded as rigidly than solids and so they flow and fill the container but don't have quite enough energy to float around in the container freely and are quite densely packed together.
